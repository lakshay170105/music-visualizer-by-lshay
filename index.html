❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about L-SHAY Visualizer

🎵 What is L-SHAY Visualizer?

L-SHAY Visualizer is a browser-based music visualization platform that allows you to create stunning music videos with 12 working templates, 15 visualizers, 30 backgrounds, 12 text animations, 10 text effects, and 8 logo effects. No installation required!

💰 Is it free to use?

Yes! L-SHAY Visualizer is completely free to use. You can create unlimited visualizations and export videos without any cost. Just create a free account to get started.

🎬 What video formats are supported?

We export videos in WebM format with VP9 codec, which offers excellent quality and compression. You can export in 720p HD resolution at 30 FPS.

🎵 What audio formats are supported?

We support all common audio formats including MP3, WAV, OGG, M4A, FLAC, and more. Simply upload your audio file and start creating!

⏱️ How long can my video be?

You can export videos of any length! Choose from:

  • 🎯 10 Second Clip: Select any 10-second segment from your audio
  • 🎯 30 Second Segment: Select any 30-second segment from your audio
  • 30 Seconds (from start): First 30 seconds of your track
  • 1 Minute: First minute of your track
  • Full Audio Length: Export your entire track

🔐 Do I need to login?

You can browse templates and use the editor without logging in, but you need to create a free account to export videos. This helps us provide better service and save your preferences.

💾 How large are the exported videos?

File size depends on duration and content. Typical sizes: 720p/30fps = ~8-12 MB/min. We show an estimate before export.

🎨 Can I customize templates?

Absolutely! Every template is fully customizable. You can change colors, visualizers, backgrounds, text effects, logo effects, and all other settings after applying a template.

📱 Does it work on mobile?

Yes! L-SHAY Visualizer is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. However, for best performance and video export, we recommend using a desktop or laptop.

🌐 Which browsers are supported?

We support all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera. For best performance, we recommend using the latest version of Chrome or Firefox.

🔧 Common Issues & Solutions

🎵 Audio not playing?

  • Make sure your audio file is in a supported format (MP3, WAV, OGG, etc.)
  • Check if your browser has permission to play audio
  • Try refreshing the page and uploading the file again

🎬 Export not working?

  • Make sure you're logged in to your account
  • Ensure you have uploaded an audio file
  • Check if your browser supports video recording (Chrome/Firefox recommended)
  • Try using a lower resolution if export fails

🐌 Performance issues?

  • Close other browser tabs and applications
  • Try using a simpler visualizer type
  • Reduce canvas resolution in settings
  • Use Chrome or Firefox for best performance

📱 Mobile issues?

  • Video export may not work on all mobile browsers
  • For best results, use desktop/laptop for export
  • You can still browse templates and preview on mobile
